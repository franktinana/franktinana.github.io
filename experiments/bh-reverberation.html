Experiment

Black Holes — Reverberation Residuals

RMAGNConsistency

Objective

Use BH masses anchored by reverberation mapping to evaluate residuals under our framework.

Method (High-Level)

  1. Load RM anchor set (BH masses, lags).
  2. Generate model expectations for chosen radii (ISCO/light-ring proxies).
  3. Compute signed proxy residuals; summarize by mass and Eddington ratio bins.
  4. Inspect outliers and update assumptions.

Key Relations (schematic)

$$ r = (Q_{\mathrm{obs}}-Q_{\mathrm{model}})/\sigma_Q $$

What does this mean?

  • We test whether BH systems line up with model expectations set by independent mass anchors.
  • Small, structureless residuals → consistency; patterns → where physics or data curation needs work.
