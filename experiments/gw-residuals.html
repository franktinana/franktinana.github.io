Experiment

Gravitational Waves — Timing Residuals

PTA/LIGOResidualsModel Check

Objective

Compare predicted vs observed timing residuals under our model and quantify mismatches.

Method (High-Level)

  1. Assemble dataset of timing residuals.
  2. Generate model predictions with same cadence and noise model.
  3. Compute signed proxy residuals; summarize by band/source.
  4. Visualize and record falsification metrics.

Key Formula

Residual definition (schematic):

$$ r_i = (t_i^{\mathrm{obs}} - t_i^{\mathrm{model}}) / \sigma_i $$

What does this mean?

  • We ask: “does the model predict the same wiggles in time as the data?”
  • If residuals cluster around zero with expected scatter, the model is consistent.
  • Structured residuals point to missing physics or wrong assumptions.
